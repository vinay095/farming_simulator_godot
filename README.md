# farming simulator

Start your new life on a peaceful island where you can grow crops, catch fish, build automated machines, and defend your precious harvest from mischievous blobs.

## Features

* **Farming**: Till the soil, plant seeds (Tomatoes, Corn, Pumpkins, and Wheat), water them, and watch them grow!
* **Resource Gathering**: Chop trees for wood and apples. Don't worry, trees regrow after 3 days!
* **Fishing Minigame**: Cast your line into the ocean and test your reflexes to catch fish.
* **Base Building & Automation**: Construct Sprinklers to water your crops, Fishers to catch fish passively, and Scarecrows to defend your farm.
* **Combat**: Swing your sword to fend off blobs that try to eat your crops.
* **Shops & Customization**: Trade your resources with NPCs for new hats and powerful machine blueprints.
* **Dynamic Weather & Day/Night Cycle**: Sleep in your bed to advance the day. Check the TV for tomorrow's weather—if it rains, your crops will get watered automatically!

## Controls

### Movement
* **W / A / S / D** or **Arrow Keys**: Move your character.

### Tools & Interaction
* **Q / E**: Cycle through your tools (Axe, Hoe, Watering Can, Seed, Sword, Fishing Rod).
* **Space**: Use current tool / Interact with objects, NPCs, and menus.
* **C**: Cycle seed type (when the Seed tool is active).
* **T**: Toggle between unlocked hat styles.

### Machines
* **M**: Enter / Exit build mode.
* **Q / E**: Cycle machine selection (while in build mode).
* **Space**: Place selected machine at your cursor.

### Other
* **N**: Toggle plant health/growth overlay.
* **Escape**: Close the shop (when inside a shop).


This game is made with the [Godot Engine](https://godotengine.org/).

1. Download and install **Godot 4.x**
2. Clone or download this repository.
3. Open the Godot Engine and import the `project.godot` file.

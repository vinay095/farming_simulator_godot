========================================
	   Pixel Fields - HOW TO PLAY
========================================

--- WELCOME ---
	You've just arrived at your new farm! Your goal is to
	build a thriving homestead — grow crops, raise resources,
	buy upgrades, and defend your farm from pesky blobs.

	You start with some basic supplies: Wood, Apples, Fish,
	and a few SEEDS for each crop type (Tomato, Corn,
	Pumpkin, and Wheat). Use these to get started!


--- CONTROLS ---

	MOVEMENT
		W / Up Arrow      Move up
		A / Left Arrow    Move left
		S / Down Arrow    Move down
		D / Right Arrow   Move right

	TOOLS
		Q                 Cycle tools backward
		E                 Cycle tools forward
		Space             Use current tool / Interact with objects
		C                 Cycle seed type (when Seed tool is selected)
		T                 Toggle between unlocked hat styles

	MACHINES
		M                 Enter / Exit build mode
		Q / E             Cycle machine selection (while in build mode)
		Space             Place selected machine at cursor

	OTHER
		N                 Toggle plant health/growth overlay
		Escape            Close the shop (when inside a shop)


--- TOOLS ---

	AXE
		Use on trees to chop them. Each hit weakens the tree
		and may knock down an apple. After enough hits the tree
		falls, leaving a stump, and you receive wood.

	HOE
		Use on grassy farmable tiles to till the soil. You need
		tilled soil before you can plant anything.

	WATER
		Use on tilled soil to water it. Crops only grow on
		watered soil when you sleep and advance the day. If it
		rains, all tilled soil gets watered automatically.

	SEED
		Use on tilled, empty soil to plant the currently
		selected seed. Press C to cycle through seed types:
		Tomato, Corn, Pumpkin, and Wheat. Planting a seed
		costs 1 of that crop from your inventory.

	SWORD
		Swing at blobs (enemies) that approach your crops.
		Each hit pushes them back and deals damage. Three hits
		will destroy a blob.

	FISHING ROD
		Use while facing a water tile (not grass/soil) to
		start the fishing minigame.


--- HOW TO FARM (STEP BY STEP) ---

	1. Walk up to a grass tile and use the HOE to till soil.
	2. Use the WATER can on the tilled soil (or let rain do it).
	3. Select the SEED tool, press C to pick a crop, then
		use it on the watered soil to plant.
	4. Go inside your house and interact with the BED (press
		Space while facing it) to sleep and advance to the
		next day.
	5. Each new day, watered crops grow one stage. Crops that
		were NOT watered will decay — if they decay too many
		days in a row, they die!
	6. When a crop is fully grown, simply walk over it to
		harvest. You'll receive 2-4 of that crop item.

	IMPORTANT: Planting costs 1 seed from your inventory.
	Make sure you have enough of the crop you want to plant!


--- HOW TO FISH ---

	1. Equip the FISHING ROD (cycle tools with Q/E).
	2. Face a water tile (any tile without grass on it).
	3. Press Space to cast your line — the fishing minigame
		will appear.
	4. A progress bar and a bouncing fish icon will show up.
		Press Space repeatedly to move your catch bar upward.
	5. Keep the fish icon INSIDE the catch bar to fill the
		progress meter. If you let it escape, the meter drops.
	6. Fill the bar to 100% to catch a fish! If it drops to
		0%, you lose the fish.


--- TREES & REGROWTH ---

	Trees can be chopped by hitting them repeatedly with the
	AXE. After about 3 hits, the tree is fully chopped down
	and turns into a stump. You'll get wood and possibly
	apples along the way.

	DON'T WORRY — chopped trees REGROW! After 3 in-game days
	(sleeping in the bed), the stump disappears and a brand
	new tree takes its place, complete with fresh apples.
	So chop freely and come back later


--- MACHINES ---

	Press M to enter build mode. A preview icon follows your
	cursor showing where the machine will be placed. Use Q/E
	to cycle machines, Space to place, and M again to exit.

	SPRINKLER
		Automatically waters all 8 surrounding soil tiles (and
		its own tile) each day. Great for hands-free farming!
		Cost: 30 Tomatoes + 20 Wheat

	FISHER
		Place near water (on a coast tile). It will automatically
		catch fish over time — you can see the progress bar
		filling up. Each time it fills, you get 1 fish.
		Cost: 25 Wood + 15 Fish

	SCARECROW
		Automatically shoots projectiles at nearby blobs to
		protect your crops. Has a limited range.
		Cost: 15 Pumpkins + 15 Corn

	DELETE
		Select this "machine" to remove a placed machine. Walk
		up to the machine you want to delete and press Space.
		Always available — no cost.


--- SHOPS & UPGRADES ---

	There are NPC characters on the map. Walk up to one and
	press Space to talk. Keep pressing Space to cycle through
	their dialog. After the dialog ends, their shop opens.

	HAT SHOP
		Buy new hat styles using your harvested items. Each hat
		is purely cosmetic. Press T in normal mode to cycle
		through your unlocked hats.

	MACHINE SHOP
		Unlock new machine types. Once unlocked, they appear
		in your build mode rotation (M key).

	Press Escape to close a shop when you're done.
	When all items in a shop are purchased, the NPC will
	show a special "finished" message instead.


--- BLOBS (ENEMIES) ---

	Blobs spawn periodically and move toward your crops.
	If a blob reaches a plant, it will damage it! Too much
	damage and the plant dies.

	You can fight blobs with:
		* SWORD — swing at them (3 hits to kill)
		* SCARECROW — automatically shoots nearby blobs

	Blobs explode when defeated.


--- HOUSE ---

	Walk into your house and the roof becomes transparent so
	you can see inside. Inside you'll find:

		BED — Interact with it (Space) to sleep and advance to
			the next day. All daily events trigger: crops grow
			or decay, machines activate, rain may come, and
			trees may regrow.

		TV  — Interact with it to check TOMORROW'S weather
			forecast. If it shows rain, all your tilled soil
			will be watered automatically the next day — you
			can skip manual watering!


--- TIPS ---

	* Check the TV every day — if rain is coming, save your
		energy and skip watering!
	* Rain waters ALL tilled soil, not just planted tiles.
	* Place sprinklers early to reduce daily chores.
	* Protect valuable crops with scarecrows — blobs can
		undo days of progress!
	* Trees regrow every 3 days — don't worry about running
		out of wood.
	* Build fishers near water to generate passive fish income.
	* You start with 1 Wood, 3 Apples, 4 Fish, and 5 of each
		starting Seed type — plant them carefully!

	========================================
			Have fun farming! 🌱
